Frequently Asked Questions

How do I install your Chrome extensions?

Our extensions can be installed directly from the Chrome Web Store. Simply click the "Download Now" button on any extension page, and you'll be directed to the store where you can add it to your browser with a single click.

Are your extensions free to use?

We offer both free and premium versions of our extensions. The free versions include core functionality, while premium versions offer additional features and capabilities. You can see the pricing details on each extension's page.

How do you handle user data and privacy?

We take privacy seriously. Our extensions only access the data they need to function, and we never sell or share your personal information. All data processing is done in accordance with our privacy policy, which you can review on our website.

Can I use your extensions on browsers other than Chrome?

Currently, our extensions are available exclusively for Google Chrome. However, we're actively working on versions for Firefox, Edge, and other browsers. Sign up for our newsletter to be notified when these become available.

How can I suggest a new feature or report a bug?

We welcome your feedback! You can suggest features or report bugs through the contact form on this page, or by emailing us directly at support@aiextensions.com. We review all suggestions and continuously work to improve our products.
