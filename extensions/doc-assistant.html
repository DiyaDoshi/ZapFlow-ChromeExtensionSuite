Frequently Asked Questions

Is Doc Assistant free to use?

Doc Assistant offers both free and premium plans. The free plan includes basic document support with limited generations per day, while the premium plan unlocks unlimited usage and advanced editing features.

What is Doc Assistant, and how does it work?

Doc Assistant is an AI-powered tool designed to help you draft, edit, and improve documents efficiently. It assists with grammar correction, formatting suggestions, rewriting content, and even generating new ideas.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. Doc Assistant ensures top-tier security and does not store or share any of your document data. Your content remains private and confidential.

What AI model powers Doc Assistant?

Doc Assistant uses a cutting-edge large language model, optimized specifically for writing and editing tasks to deliver accurate, relevant, and high-quality suggestions.
