Frequently Asked Questions

Is Gmail Assistant free to use?

Gmail Assistant offers both free and premium plans. The free plan allows basic writing assistance with a limited number of generations per day, while the premium plan provides unlimited generations and access to advanced features.

What is Gmail Assistant, and how does it work?

Gmail Assistant is an AI-powered tool that helps you manage your emails efficiently. It can categorize emails, suggest smart replies, schedule emails, and set reminders for important tasks.

Is my data secure?

Yes! Gmail Assistant follows strict security protocols and does not share or store sensitive information. Your emails remain private and secure.

What AI model does AI Writer use?

AI Writer uses a state-of-the-art large language model, fine-tuned specifically for writing tasks to ensure high-quality, coherent, and contextually appropriate content generation.
